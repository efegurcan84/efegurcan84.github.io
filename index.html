Welcome to Global Geopolitics

Thank you for visiting Global Geopolitics, a newly launched peer-reviewed academic journal dedicated to analyzing the complex dynamics of international relations, power structures, and global strategic trends. Our mission is to provide a platform for rigorous scholarship that advances critical debates in geopolitics, international security, and global governance.

We are currently preparing our inaugural issue and invite scholars, researchers, and practitioners to contribute original research, theoretical analyses, and policy perspectives. Submissions are now open, and we welcome articles that engage with pressing geopolitical challenges from multidisciplinary and critical perspectives.

Stay tuned for updates as we build a vibrant intellectual space for discussions on contemporary and historical geopolitical developments. If you would like to receive notifications about our upcoming publications and calls for papers, please subscribe to our mailing list.

For submission guidelines and more information, please visit our Instructions for Authors and About pages.
